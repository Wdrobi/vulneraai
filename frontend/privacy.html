Privacy Policy

How VulneraAI collects, uses, and safeguards your data.

1. Data We Collect

Account data (username, email), authentication tokens, scan inputs (targets you provide), and scan outputs (findings, risk scores). We do not collect payment data.

2. How We Use Data

To run scans you request, generate reports, provide dashboards, improve detection quality, and secure our services. We do not sell personal data.

3. Data Retention

Scan data is retained for your history and reporting. You may request deletion of your account and associated scan results. Backups observe the same retention controls.

4. Security Measures

Transport encryption (TLS), scoped authentication tokens, role-based access controls, and environment hardening protect stored data. Access to customer data is restricted to least privilege.

5. Your Rights

You can view, export, or delete your data where applicable. Contact us for privacy requests at the email on our Contact page.

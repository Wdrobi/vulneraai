Security

How we protect VulneraAI and your data.

1. Encryption

All traffic is encrypted in transit with TLS. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted with strong ciphers; keys are rotated and access is restricted.

2. Access Controls

Role-based access, least privilege, and scoped tokens protect administrative and user operations. Authentication events are monitored for anomalies.

3. Infrastructure Hardening

We patch regularly, minimize exposed services, and enforce secure baselines on hosts, containers, and networks. Backups are encrypted and tested.

4. Vulnerability Management

Continuous scanning, dependency checks, and code review reduce risk. Critical findings are prioritized and tracked to closure.

5. Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a vulnerability, contact us via the email on the Contact page. Do not publicly disclose until we have remediated.
